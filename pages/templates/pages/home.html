LaserParams

is an open-source platform that aims to unify laser technology parameters for future collaborations. With a vision of fostering innovation and knowledge sharing, LaserParams provides a centralized repository for laser-related data, specifications, and settings. This platform enables researchers, engineers, and enthusiasts to access and contribute to a comprehensive collection of laser parameters, facilitating collaboration and accelerating technological advancements.
